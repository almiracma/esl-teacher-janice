Get to Know Me

About Teacher Janice

Hello! I'm Teacher Janice, a licensed professional teacher from the Philippines with over 13 years of dedicated ESL teaching experience. My journey has taken me to international schools across Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, Bangkok, Thailand, and the Philippines, giving me a deep understanding of diverse learning styles and cultural backgrounds.

Beyond the classroom, I've also taught students from all over the world online — including learners from Japan, Vietnam, Thailand, South Korea, China, and Russia. This global experience allows me to adapt my teaching style to each student's unique cultural and linguistic background.

I believe that learning English should be enjoyable, practical, and tailored to each student's goals. Whether you're preparing for an international exam, advancing your career, or building confidence in everyday conversations, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.
